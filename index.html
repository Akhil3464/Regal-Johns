Our Leadership

Guided by our purpose, we deliver more than parcels—we deliver trust, care, and happiness.

Ranju John

Founder

Ranju John is the Founder and Director of Regal Johns International Ltd. After completing his Master’s degree at Northumbria University, United Kingdom, he established the company in Manchester with a vision to provide reliable global logistics solutions. Today, the Regal Johns group operates in both the United Kingdom and India, providing trusted international courier, cargo, and shipping services to customers worldwide. Under his leadership, the company continues to grow with a strong focus on reliability, efficiency, and customer satisfaction.

Stephy Ranju

Managing Partner

Stephy Ranju is the Managing Partner of Regal Johns International. She plays a vital role in overseeing the company's operations, business development, and strategic initiatives. With her strong understanding of logistics and customer relations, she ensures the smooth functioning of services while driving the company's growth and brand presence. Her leadership in marketing, communication, and client engagement helps strengthen Regal Johns International's global network and long-term success.

Paulson P John

Managing Partner

Paulson P. John is the Managing Partner of Regal Johns International Ltd., leading the company’s UK operations from Lincolnshire. Before joining Regal Johns, he gained valuable experience at Motherson, developing expertise in the automotive industry, exports, and logistics. Today, he oversees the company’s UK cargo and logistics operations, ensuring reliable service and operational excellence.

Our Mission, Vision & Core Values

Guided by our purpose, we deliver more than parcels - we deliver trust, care,and happiness

Our Mission

To bridge the distance between Kerala and the United Kingdom by providing reliable, secure, and affordable international courier and trade solutions. We are committed to delivering every shipment with care, transparency, and efficiency while building lasting relationships through exceptional customer service.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted Kerala-to-UK logistics and trade partner, recognized for innovation, operational excellence, and customer satisfaction. We aspire to simplify international shipping while connecting families, businesses, and communities across borders.

Our Core Values

Customer First – Every shipment is handled with dedication, care, and personalized service.

Integrity – We operate with honesty, transparency, and accountability in everything we do.

Reliability – We deliver on our promises with consistent, timely, and secure logistics solutions.

Excellence – We continuously improve our services through innovation and operational efficiency.

Global Connectivity – We strengthen connections between Kerala and the UK, making international trade and courier services simple and accessible.

Care & Responsibility – Every parcel carries value, memories, and trust, and we treat each one with the utmost respect.

Teamwork – We collaborate across borders to deliver seamless logistics experiences for our customers.
